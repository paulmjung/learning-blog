About Me

My name is Paul Matthias Jung. I'm a learning and development professional, designer, and LEGO SERIOUS PLAY facilitator.

What I've Done

I've spent the last 10 years of my professional career working at two Minnesota-grown Fortune 500 companies, primarily in training, instructional design, and coaching capacities. I'm passionate about creating experiences that will enable employees to build skills and knowledge, and in turn be more engaged and satisfied in their work.

What Inspires Me

I like to build things--real, tangible things--almost as much as I like to learn. I try to approach the world with a beginner's mind. I love symmetry and simplicity. I'm a sucker for nostalgia. I like pencils and paper. I need to be outside. I love new technology.

What I'm Learning

I'm curious. I'm learning how to take the skills I have and apply them to software and web design and development. I'm learning HTML, CSS, Javascript, and a little bit of Python. I'm learning how to commit code using GitHub and the terminal.

I'm also studying yoga.

Contact Me

If you're interested in getting in touch with me--to talk design, to learn more about LEGO SERIOUS PLAY, whatever--send me a note via email. I'm always interested in meeting other curious people.
